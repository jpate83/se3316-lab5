Click the 'DMCA tools' link on your admin dashboard to get started.

-> There will be a list of take down requests
    -> Fill in the message box and hit 'send' to send
       an infrignment notice to the owner of the
       collection that has been requested to be taken
       down.
-> Below this list, there will be a list of takedown notices
    -> A dispute message will appear if the user has disputed the notice.
    -> Click the 'take content down' button to remove the content from the site.
    -> To restore content that has a disputed infringment notice, click the 'restore content button'
